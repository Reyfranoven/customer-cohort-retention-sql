# Business Insights

## Key Findings

- The largest revenue drop occurs between Month 0 and Month 1 (average -80%).
- Revenue decay slows significantly after Month 2.
- A small portion of customers generate stable recurring revenue.

## Business Risk

Early churn represents the highest revenue risk.

## Strategic Recommendation

1. Improve first-month onboarding strategy.
2. Offer time-limited incentives within 30 days.
3. Identify and prioritize high-retention cohorts.
4. Develop loyalty programs to improve CLV.
